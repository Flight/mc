Create the theme song to Narcos season 2

Netflix hit series NARCOS are inviting creators from around the world
to design megaofficial merchandise.

Artists should consider:

  • The type of designs that work well as clothing products, not just great artwork. For example, humor driven and text-based designs tend to work particularly well in the apparel space. This could be in the form of an iconic quote from the show that fans would identify with.
  • Designs should be easily adapted to a wide range of merchandise items from hoodies, to t-shirts, to mugs etc.
  • The message and the connection it creates/emotional response is more important than illustration. Is there a real world event/trending topic you could incorporate? E.g. "Make Colombia Great Again."
  • Simplicity can be powerful, so think about what you can achieve with a 2-3 color base to create an impact.

A minimum of three Selected Designs will be produced and sold as official NARCOS merchandise via teespring.com, and the selected artists will receive USD $200 for each design and a royalty of USD $0.50 for each item sold.
