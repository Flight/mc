Pricing FAQ

What types of payment do you accept?

Currently we accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express and PayPal.

Do I have to sign a long term contract?

No. Music Clout is a pay-as-you-go service. There are no long term contracts or commitments on your part. You simply pay month-to-month or in full for annual subscription. All subscriptions can be canceled at anytime.

Can I switch plans?

Yep. Simply click on the “Account” tab on your dashboard and you’ll see your options.

How do I cancel the service?

You can cancel your subscription in your account settings, or by contacting billing@musicclout.com. Cancelling your subscription will stop it from auto-renewing on your renewal date, but you will continue to receive access for the time period that you have paid for.
